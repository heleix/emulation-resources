How to Use This Guide

Following along with the entirety of this guide is designed to take up an estimated 45-60 minutes total as if this were a synchronous lesson.

The guide is divided into separate modules, each with their own estimated lengths of time. This estimated run time will allow you to return to specific modules or spend more time with others depending on your level of understanding and background.
